Bubbles Fandom

For Cat Lovers

The Bubble's Fandom is a small local community of cat lovers on the Northshore of New Zealand!

The visions of their fandom is to create conversations for cat lovers to ask and answer questions to make their cat's lives better.

It will be a place where people can share pictures of their cats and talk about their cats. A social platform strictly for cat lovers.

This will be a one stop pitstop to all the knowledge and discussion surrounding all sort of cats.

Welcome to Bubbles Fandom

A community for cat lovers to connect, share, celebrate their love of cats.
